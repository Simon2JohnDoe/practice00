Types of Paper

There are two main types of paper

Notebook Paper

Notebook paper is mainly used by students to complete assignments.

Computer Paper

Computer paper is mainly used by people who have a printer. Sometimes people who don't have notebook paper available will use computer paper for taking notes. This isn't advisable since there are no easily seen lines so people might write at an angle wasting valuable space.
